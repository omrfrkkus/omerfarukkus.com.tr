My Hobbies

My hobbies are making sculptures and epoxy lamps from wood. I am also interested in wood carving and painting. I combine all of these to make epoxy lamps. First, I make the drawings. Then I carve the wooden pieces and make the mini sculptures inside them with clay. Then I paint the sculptures. I combine the painted sculptures with the wooden pieces. I make a mold using a water bottle or a similar object. I place what I have made into this mold and pour colored epoxy on it. After the epoxy is dry, I carve the top to create a wave effect. I sand it from 80 grit to 3000 grit. After sanding, I apply polish to make the epoxy transparent and shiny. I bend a metal pipe for the lamp. I solder the installation inside. Then I complete the lamp by combining all the parts with a strong adhesive. As a final touch, I apply wood protector to the lamp.
