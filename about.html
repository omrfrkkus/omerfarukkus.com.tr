Hello, my name is Ömer Faruk Kuş. I was born in Istanbul, Turkey. My hometown is Trabzon, Of. I was born on November 23, 2001.

I am a software student and an independent project developer. I discovered my interest in software at a young age and have been striving to improve in this field ever since. Currently, I am a final year software student and I continue my education in this field. I am particularly interested in programming languages such as Flutter, C#, and Java. I am attracted to these languages because of their multi-platform support and rapid development capabilities. Additionally, I am passionate about keeping up with the latest advancements in the mobile and computer sectors.

Street workout is another hobby that holds a significant place in my life. I discovered this sport in 2016 and have been actively involved in it ever since. I focus on streetlifting and strive to specialize in various movements. This sport challenges me physically while helping me improve my strength, endurance, and flexibility. Street workout is not just a sport for me, it is a lifestyle. Regularly engaging in my workouts motivates me and keeps me determined to achieve my goals.

Sculpting is a hobby that provides me with a creative outlet. I use clay to create various sculptures and enhance them by combining them with epoxy for durability. I also have experience in creating decorative objects such as accessories and night lamps. Sculpting serves as a therapeutic activity for me. It allows me to explore my creativity, pay attention to details, and create aesthetically pleasing works.

Traveling is a passion of mine as well. Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and witnessing natural beauties excite me. I particularly enjoy trekking and camping in the midst of nature. I have had the opportunity to explore the Aegean and Black Sea regions in Turkey. I have also visited countries like Italy and Poland, where I have been exposed to different cultures and lifestyles. My goal is to visit countries renowned for their natural beauty, such as Norway, and to embark on more travel experiences.

I share content related to these hobbies on my website. I showcase my software projects and applications, street workout progress and techniques, and my sculptures. These hobbies have a positive impact on me and provide me with motivation. When I create something beautiful, it brings me joy and inspires me to embark on new projects.
